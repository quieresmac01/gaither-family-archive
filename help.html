How to Use This Site

Navigation

Moving Between Sections: Click on the tabs at the top of each page to switch between:

  • Catalog - Browse all photos in the archive
  • Slideshow - View photos automatically one after another
  • Family Messages - Read and share memories about the photos

Return Home: Click the "← Home" button at the top left of any page to return to the main page.

Browsing the Catalog

Searching for Photos:

  • Type words in the search box to find specific photos (example: "Christmas", "house", "letter")
  • Click "Clear" to see all photos again

Viewing Photos:

  • Click on any photo to see it larger
  • Use the "Previous" and "Next" buttons to move between photos
  • Click the "×" or press ESC on your keyboard to close the large view

Changing Pages:

  • Use "Previous Page" and "Next Page" buttons at the bottom
  • Change how many photos appear per page using the dropdown menu

Using the Slideshow

Starting and Stopping:

  • Click "Play" to start the slideshow
  • Click "Pause" to stop it
  • Or press the Spacebar on your keyboard to play/pause

Changing Speed:

  • Use the "Transition Speed" dropdown to choose how fast photos change
  • Options: Fast (2 seconds), Medium (4 seconds), Slow (6 seconds), Very Slow (10 seconds)

Shuffle Mode:

  • Click "Shuffle: OFF" to show photos in random order
  • Click "Shuffle: ON" to return to numbered order (1, 2, 3...)

Fullscreen Mode:

  • Click the "Fullscreen" button to fill your entire screen with photos
  • Press F on your keyboard or click the × button to exit fullscreen
  • You can also press ESC to exit fullscreen

Sharing Memories

Reading Messages:

  • Scroll down to see all family messages and memories
  • Use the search box to find messages by name or topic

Adding Your Own Message:

  • Fill in your name (example: "J. Gaither")
  • Write your memory or comment in the message box
  • If your message is about a specific photo, type the photo number (example: "1234.jpg")
  • Click "Post Message" to share

Why Share? Your memories help everyone find photos! When you describe what's in a photo, others can search for those words and discover it.

Helpful Tips

  • Keyboard Shortcuts:
    • Spacebar = Play/Pause slideshow
    • F = Toggle fullscreen mode
    • ESC = Exit fullscreen or close large photo view
  • Best for Large Screens: The slideshow and fullscreen modes work best on computers, tablets, and large phones
  • Take Your Time: There's no rush - explore at your own pace!

Questions? Ask a family member for help, or return to the home page.
